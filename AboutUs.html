Welcome to Sri LakshmiNarasimar Temple

Nestled in the serene surroundings of Salem, Tamil Nadu, Sri LakshmiNarasimar Temple stands as a beacon of spiritual solace and divine grace. Our temple is dedicated to Lord Narasimha, an avatar of Lord Vishnu, and is renowned for its stunning architecture, intricate carvings, and tranquil ambiance.

Our mission is to foster a deep sense of devotion and community among our followers. We offer various spiritual services, including daily poojas, special rituals, and community events that bring people together in the spirit of faith and unity.

The temple is a hub of cultural and spiritual activities, hosting numerous festivals and events that capture the essence of our rich traditions and vibrant community spirit. From the grand celebrations of Narasimha Jayanti to the serene rituals of Lakshmi Puja, each event is a testament to our devotion and unity.
